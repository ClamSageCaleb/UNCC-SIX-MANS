Norm's Language

General Commands

  • !q: adds you to the queue for 60 minutes
  • !q 30: adds you to the queue for 30 minutes
    • Queue times are intervals of 10 with a minimum of 10 minutes and a maximum of 60 minutes.
    • At 5 minutes left, a warning message is sent.
    • Another message is sent when the player is removed.
  • !qq: adds you to the queue for 60 minutes without pinging everyone
  • !leave: removes you from the queue
  • !flip: 50/50 chance of joining or leaving the queue
  • !list: lists the players currently in the queue
  • !brokenq: destroys the latest popped queue

Once the Queue Pops (once the queue has 6 people)

  • !random: randomly assigns teams
  • !captains: chooses two captains to pick teams manually

Captains Mode

Blue captain will pick first and pick ONE player:

  • !pick [mention player]

After the blue captain picks a player, the orange captain will pick TWO people:

  • !pick [mention player 1] [mention player 2]

The last player will be automatically added to the Blue team

Once the Series Ends

One player from each team will report the winner of the series using:

  • !report [winning team color]
You cannot queue again until the match has been reported by both teams.

The Leaderboard

  • !leaderboard: shows the top 5 on the leaderboard
    • !leaderboard me: shows your rank on the leaderboard
    • !leaderboard [mention player]: shows mentioned player's rank on the leaderboard
You must play 5 series before being added to the leaderboard.
